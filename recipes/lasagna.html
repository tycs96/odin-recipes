Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 Pasta Sheets
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 16 ources ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound cooked meat of your choice

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 375
  2. pour both 1 can of tomato sauce and tomato paste into a glass baking container
  3. mix the sause and paste evenly
  4. add 1/2 pound of cooked meet evenly on top
  5. add 8 ounces of ricotta cheese in a layer on top
  6. place 1 pasta sheet
  7. pour both 3/4 can of tomato sauce and tomato paste into a glass baking container
  8. mix the sauce and paste evenly
  9. add 1/4 pound of cooked meet evenly spread
  10. add 8 ounces of ricotta cheese in a layer on top
  11. sprinkle 1/2 pound of mozzarella cheese
  12. place 1 pasta sheet
  13. pour 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese on top of the fresh pasta sheet
  14. add remaining meat on top
  15. place last pasta sheet on top
  16. pour remaining tomato paste and sauce on top
  17. sprinkle all remaining cheese on top
  18. place container into the oven covered with foil
  19. remove foil after 30 minutes
  20. continue baking for 20 minutes or until desired golden coloring for the cheese on top
